Football data is most useful when it helps you see what could happen next.

I built **Touchline 26**, an independent analytics experience for the 2026 World Cup. It brings the tournament into one focused, visual workspace:

• Live scores and match state  
• Modeled result probabilities and projected xG  
• Group qualification simulations  
• Interactive knockout scenarios  
• Side-by-side team comparisons

The app runs on Cloudflare, uses public match data, and calculates its projections directly in the browser. It is designed to be fast, transparent, and useful on any screen.
